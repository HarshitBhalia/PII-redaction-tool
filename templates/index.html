How It Works

Our hybrid 4-layer pipeline ensures maximum detection accuracy

🧠

Layer 1: NER Model

🔍

Layer 3: Custom Regex

Specialized patterns for Indian PAN, Aadhaar, phone numbers, passports, and regional identifiers
